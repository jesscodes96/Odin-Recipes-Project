Brownies

Description

A delicious dessert to pair with any meal.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Baking Powder
  • Egg
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Add dry ingredients together and mix in bowl
  2. Mix in wet ingredients until no podwer is remaining
  3. Pour into 9x9 pan and bake
  4. Cut into squares and enjoy!
