Spaghetti

Description

A plate of delicious spaghetti, smothered in tomato sauce and topped with cheese.
This dish is easy to prepara and is a classic favourite for any family. Good meal after any long day at work.

Ingredients

  • spaghetti noodles
  • tomato sauce
  • meatballs(optional)
  • parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil noodles and let cook 12 minutes
  2. If adding frozen meatballs, this is a good time to cook them as the noodles cook
  3. Heat up tomato sauce on low setting in small saucepan as spaghetti cooks
  4. Once noodles are done, drain in colander or strainer and place back in pot
  5. Add tomato sauce and mix together
  6. Serve in pasta bowl and add meatballs and cheese
  7. Enjoy!
