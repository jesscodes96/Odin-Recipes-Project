Pizza

Description

A favourite dish for anyone to enjoy, it's simple and offers multiple topping options.
Best for parties and weekends at home.

Ingredients

  • pizza crust
  • pizza sauce
  • cheese
  • any topping your heart desires

Steps

  1. Put sauce on pizza crust
  2. Top with lots of cheese
  3. Add favourite toppings
  4. Bake until crust is brown around edges, longer for a crispier crust.
  5. Enjoy!
